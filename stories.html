Other Stories

While the gods and the creation story lie at the center of most religious systems, there are always other, more individual tales and beings that hold importance. Here is the chance to explore a few that the Maya believed.

*This is only a brief introduction to some of the stories/concepts within Mayan culture. There are countless more of varying levels of accuracy, and the ones listed here are a lot more complex than described.
