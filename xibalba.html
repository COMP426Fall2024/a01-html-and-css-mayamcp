Xibalba: The Mayan Underworld

Xibalba, roughly translated as "place of fright", is the name of the underworld in Mayan mythology. It is ruled by twelve powerful beings deemed the Lords of Xibalba, malevolent beings who test and challenge the souls of the deceased. Of these 12, there are two death gods: Hun-Came ("One Death") and Vucub-Came ("Seven Death"). The other ten are lesser but still powerful beings, akin to demons. They each control various methods of human suffering and death.
